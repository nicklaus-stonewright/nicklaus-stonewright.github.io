Welcome to a FizzBuzz Game by Owsama in JS

Players generally sit in a circle. The player designated to go first says the number "1", and the players then count upwards in turn. However:

  • Any number divisible by 3 is replaced by the word Fizz
  • Any number divisible by 5 by the word Buzz
  • Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 become FizzBuzz
