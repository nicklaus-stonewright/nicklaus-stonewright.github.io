Welcome to a FizzBuzz Game by Nicklaus in JS

Click on Start Game. The player must then click on one of the buttons depending on the value of the number shown:

  • Any number divisible by 3 is replaced by the word Fizz
  • Any number divisible by 5 by the word Buzz
  • Numbers divisible by 3 and 5 become FizzBuzz
  • All others numbers become Pass
